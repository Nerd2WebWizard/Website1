Daily Life

A light breakfast would be consumed before heading to work in a typical Roman day. Early in the afternoon, when work was finished, many Romans would quickly travel to the baths to take a bath and socialise. They would have dinner at around 3 o'clock, which was more of a social gathering than a meal.

Why They Were Famous/What They Were Known For

The ancient Romans were renowned for their political, military, and social structures. They also constructed several highways and aqueducts as well as spreading Latin, their native language, throughout much of Europe and northern Africa.

Dates of Existence

After the Stone Age and Bronze Age, the Iron Age was a time in human history that began between 1200 and 600 B.C., depending on where it occurred. People across a substantial chunk of Europe, Asia, and areas of Africa began producing tools and weapons out of iron and steel during the Iron Age.

Tools

Iron and steel were used extensively in construction and for tools and weapons. Fire welding was often used to add pieces of steel to make the edges of tools and weapons, which could be heat-treated by quenching to harden them.

Interesting Facts

Many objects were created by the Romans. Gladiator battles were the most well-liked type of entertainment in ancient Rome. The wealthy Romans used servants. Several of the Roman roads are still in use. They revered a wide variety of Gods and Goddesses. Ancient Rome was underground.
